About me

WHat I do

I'm Shri Nikesh, an industrial design student graduating in 2026, passionate about crafting thoughtful, intuitive, and functional product experiences.

My work lies at the intersection of electronics, product design, and user experience bridging physical form with digital thinking. I focus on creating seamless, meaningful interactions through human-centered design.

Outside of design, I’m drawn to street and nature photography, where I explore raw moments, textures, and environments. It sharpens my eye for detail, composition, and storytelling skills that deeply influence my design perspective.

My background

Based in Chennai, India. I’m an Industrial Designer with a multidisciplinary edge, working across product design, packaging, and UI/UX. I’ve collaborated with health startups, research institutes, and consumer brands, building solutions rooted in usability, storytelling, and refined form.

My approach

Design, to me, is about empathy and intention. I observe closely, ask the right questions, and design with clarity creating objects and experiences that feel intuitive, bold, and honest.
